What is

SEE THE CATALOG AND FIND OUT MORE ABOUT THE USE OF EACH LABEL TYPE

Offset label


The most affordable and frequently used type of product. Used in laser and inkjet printers for both black and white and color printing.

Ribbon (Coloring Label)


Used to apply information to the label. Used with a semi-gloss label. The applied print can withstand high / low temperatures, high humidity, aggressive environments and various weather conditions.

Thermal label


The most popular product on the label market, used by grocery, construction and other supermarkets, as well as large enterprises for inventory control of products. This type of label requires a special thermal transfer printer.

Semi-glossy label


This type of label is used for warehouse accounting of long-term storage goods (warehouses, refrigerators). Retains its properties for a long time and under unfavorable conditions (high / low temperature, high humidity, high resistance to abrasion).
